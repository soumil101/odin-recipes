Quesadilla

Description

Simple. No need for explanation, lets jump right in.

Main Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Your Favorite Cheese, grated

Yup, that's really it. Let's cook!

Steps

  1. Put tortilla on a pan on the stovetop, use medium heat
  2. Sprinkle cheese on half of tortilla
  3. Fold over tortilla onto cheese
  4. Let cook for about a minute, flip over. The cooked side of tortilla should be crsipy, and golden brown
  5. Let other side cook for about a minute. Remove from heat
  6. Let cool down. Cheese should be melted
  7. Enjoy!!!
