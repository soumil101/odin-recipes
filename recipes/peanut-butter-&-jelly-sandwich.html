Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Description

A college classic. Let's get straight to it.

Ingredients

Should be pretty self-explanatory but here's the list

  • Bread slices
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Directions

  1. Spread peanute butter on one side of bread using a knife
  2. Spread jelly on the other side, rinse knife before to avoid cross contamination
  3. Put two slices of bread together
  4. Enjoy!!!
